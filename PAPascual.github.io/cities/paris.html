Paris, France

Paris, the capital of France, is a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. The city's romantic ambiance and rich history make it a perennial favorite for travelers worldwide with internationally-acclaimed destinations such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral draw millions of visitors annually.

Why I Want To Go There

One of my original "Want to go" places stemming from my childhood. Watching movies like 100 Dalmatians, Pacific Rim, and other moves that used this city as a setting made want to visit the City of Love one day.

How To Get There?

Being such a popular place, many modes of transportations are at your disposal to use. With 2 international airports and trains connecting it to the other European countries, it is a very accessible town.
