London, United Kingdom

London, the capital of England and the United Kingdom, is a historic city with a modern edge. A global hub for culture, finance, and the arts, London offers a diverse and captivating experience. Its iconic landmarks, such as the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and the British Museum, attract visitors worldwide.

Why I Want To Go There

I think for me, United Kingdom became my newest America for me. Since my want of travelling to America has faded, I've slowly wanted to experience European culture more. And London is such a go-to destination for me.

How To Get There?

London is such a megacenter for basically everything so many airports are commercially available almost at any place there such as Heathrow and Gatwick airport. There are also train routes connecting London to Paris and Brussels through a deep underground tunnel.
