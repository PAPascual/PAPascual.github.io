Vocaloid

A genre of music where producers use voicebanks (a synthesized version of a person's voice) to sing the melodies of their songs!

kyu-kurarin

Artist: iyowa ft. KAFU

Album: Single; WATASHI NO HERITAGE

Release Year: 2021

My most favorite Vocaloid song! I remember very vividly all the times I listened to this song due to it's hypnotic melody and beautiful lyrics~

The Peachy Key

Artist: iyowa ft. Hatsune Miku

Album: Films, Sunny Spots, Graduations

Release Year: 2024

Similar to kyu-kurarin, this song's melancholic melody and mesmerizing instrumental hooked me! But once I understood the lyrics, I can't help but cry to this song all the time ^^

Venom

Artist: kairikibear ft. flower

Album: Single

Release Year: 2018

The first song that got me into Vocaloid! Back then, a popular style were these powerful and rock-type songs that talked about various social issues and this song was a standout during its year!

J-Pop

Pop songs made by Japanese artists! When I started learning Japanese back in highschool, these songs were at the forefront of enhancing my literacy.

Only Wonder

Artist: frederic

Album: Frederhythm

Release Year: 2016

During my period of playing osu!, this song was the most fun to play due to its simple yet charming melody!

GHOST

Artist: Hoshimachi Suisei

Album: Single

Release Year: 2021

Hoshimachi Suisei is a Japanese VTuber, a kind of content creator that uses a virtual avatar for their activities. This song was about her journey as a singer and how she wishes to never be forgotten by her fans and for the world to notice her talent.

Venom

Artist: Yoh Kamiyama

Album: SHIAWASE NO OTONA

Release Year: 2019

The song's charm for me comes with its very catchy chorus and it's ability to play around the words well enough to make a beautiful flow.

Game OST

Songs made to specifically enhance the experience of certain games. While these songs become mostly unnoticed, when one song hits different it becomes a truly amazing experience.

Fly, My Wings

Artist: Mili

Album: Single

Release Year: 2023

This song was featured in one of the main climactic moments in the game Limbus Company. When the final part of the song dropped during the cutscene, tears fell and it was that moment that solidified my love for the game.

String Theocracy

Artist: Mili

Album: To Kill a Living Book -For Library of Ruina-

Release Year: 2021

The opening song of the game Library of Ruina. The song's complex lyrics talk about how one wishes to be free after being tugged on by strings for so long.

My Time

Artist: bo en

Album: Pale Machine

Release Year: 2013

A song used in the ending scene in the game OMORI in 2020. The song nor the game are for the light-hearted. As they both talk about the distresses of despair.
